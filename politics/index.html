Politics

Another field I’m interested in besides data is (Dutch) politics. I’ve been a member of the Dutch Liberal party VVD since I was 18. Almost immediately after joining the party I took up a board membership at the local department and I’ve been active ever since.

Whether we like it or not politics have a huge influence on our society and everyday lives. I hope that by helping the party I can support it in making The Netherlands even more prosperous than today and make sure that everyone is accepted in our beautiful nation.

Don’t hesitate to contact me if you want to know more about the Liberal party or want to become political active in a local municipality.
