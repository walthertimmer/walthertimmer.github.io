Through my economics studies, I encountered data and the various things you can do and infer from it. This sparked my interest, and after my studies, I entered the data world by working for a consultancy firm, Hot ITem in Amsterdam.

In my work, I focus on building data platforms for larger companies in the Netherlands. This involves both designing the appropriate architecture and tools, as well as implementing these platforms and the data products.

On this website, I hope to share thoughts on certain developments in the data field. This could be about new techniques, new tools, or new insights.
